Liver Sausage Pineapple

Description:

From 1953's Better Homes and Gardens New Cookbook, this is liver sausage covered in Jell-O and mayo, molded into a pineapple shape and "studded" with olives.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound liver sausage
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons unflavored gelatine
  • 1 cup mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Mix 1 pound liver sausage with 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce, 1/4 cup mayonnaise.
  2. Shape around a jelly glass.
  3. Soften 2 teaspoons unflavored gelatine in 2 tablespoons cold water, dissolve over hot water; add 1 cup mayonnaise; chill.
  4. Frost “pineapple,” score; stud with sliced stuffed olives.
  5. Top with real pineapple top.
